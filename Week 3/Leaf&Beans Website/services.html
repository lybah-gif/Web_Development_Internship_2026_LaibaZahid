What we offer, and what's on the menu

Beyond a place to sit and read, Leaf & Beans runs a few services regulars ask about most — plus the full list of what's on the shelves and the counter.

Book Trade-In

Bring in books you've finished with and trade them for store credit toward anything on our shelves.

  • 3 books in good condition ≈ 1 book of store credit
  • Staff review happens while you order a coffee
  • No appointment needed

Book Club & Events Hosting

We host a monthly book club, occasional poetry nights, and can open the back room for private reading groups.

  • Monthly book club — first Thursday, free to join
  • Private bookings for groups of 8–20
  • We'll suggest a title if your group needs one

Custom Gift Boxes

Tell us a bit about who it's for and we'll build a small box: one or two books, a bag of coffee, and something sweet.

  • Ready within 24 hours
  • Gift wrapping included
  • Local delivery available

Reading Café Space

No minimum spend to sit down, free wifi, and quiet hours every weekday morning before 11am for anyone who needs to focus.

  • Quiet hours: Mon–Fri, 8–11am
  • Free refills on hot water for tea
  • Plug sockets at every window seat
